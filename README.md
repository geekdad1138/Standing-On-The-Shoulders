# Standing on the Shoulders

A love letter and map to the founders of computing and AI.

## The Idea

We didn't get here by accident. We got here step by step, through people who didn't know they were changing the world yet. 

This isn't a Wikipedia clone. It's not a textbook. It's a **lineage**. A **provenance**. Why the buttons mattered, not just who pressed them.

## No Breakthroughs. Just Stacked Insights.

We tell ourselves stories about lone geniuses. A single person in a lab, alone with an idea, suddenly inventing the future. That's a myth.

Every breakthrough was built on inherited ideas. Every innovator stood on the shoulders of those who came before. This project makes that visible.

## Why This Matters

If you want to understand modern AI, you need to understand not just *what* changed, but *why* and *how*. 

- Why the Dartmouth Conference mattered.
- What the Turing Test was really asking.
- How "Attention is All You Need" changed everything.

This is a way to learn the names of the people who got us here and to honor the lineage of ideas that define our world.

## A Work in Progress

We are actively building this map. Content is being added, and connections are being drawn. This project celebrates the founders by showing how we truly did get here together.
